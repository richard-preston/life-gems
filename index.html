Life Gems

In college, my capstone course was called "Senior Design". Besides working on our projects, the students' weekly assignment was to watch or read some material listed by the professor. These were called the "design gems" for the powerful, critical ideas they contained. This website offers "life gems" – content I find valuable and want to share with others. I hope you derive some truth or joy from these videos, songs, quotes, and more.
